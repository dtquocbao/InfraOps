## 1. Purpose

This procedure establishes mandatory lockout-tagout (LOTO) requirements for all Meridian Grid Services personnel and subcontractors working at Substation Alpha. It applies to electrical, mechanical, and hydraulic energy sources rated above 50 volts or capable of causing injury through unexpected energization.

## 2. Authorized Personnel

Only employees who have completed MGS LOTO training (course SAF-101, 8 hours) and hold a current authorization card may perform lockout. Substation Alpha site-specific training (SAF-101A, 2 hours) is required before first LOTO application on site.

## 3. Procedure Steps

1. **Notify** affected personnel and Helix Power Cooperative dispatch (555-0142) of planned LOTO.
2. **Identify** all energy sources using the equipment-specific LOTO sheet (located in control building CB-01, binder LOTO-138).
3. **Shut down** equipment using normal operating controls; verify zero load on MPT-1 and MPT-2 before opening disconnects.
4. **Isolate** energy sources by opening visible break disconnects DS-101 through DS-108; verify blade separation visually.
5. **Apply locks** - each worker applies their personal red lock (MGS serial prefix RL-) to every isolation point. One lock per worker per device minimum.
6. **Apply tags** with name, date, time, and reason for lockout. Tags must be legible and weather-resistant.
7. **Release stored energy** - bleed hydraulic control circuits, discharge capacitor banks CB-CAP-01 and CB-CAP-02 (wait 5 minutes minimum), block mechanical springs on GIS disconnects.
8. **Verify zero energy** using calibrated multimeter (Fluke 87V or equivalent) on all three phases; test before touch.
9. **Perform work** within scope of the approved work permit (MGS Form WP-003).
10. **Restore** - remove tools, replace guards, remove locks in reverse order (last on, first off), notify dispatch before re-energization.

## 4. Prohibited Actions

Never remove another worker's lock. Never bypass interlocks. Group lockout boxes (GLB-01 at Substation Alpha yard entrance) are required when more than 5 workers are on the same isolation.
