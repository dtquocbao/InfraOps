## Section 1 - Project Description

Helix Power Cooperative ("Owner") solicits engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) services for Substation Alpha, a 138/13.8 kV substation with two 50 MVA transformers, 138 kV GIS switchyard, and 2.8-mile transmission line interconnection to Westfield Substation. Target commercial operation date (COD) is 2027-03-31.

## Section 2 - Scope of Work

The selected EPC contractor shall deliver:

- Complete detailed engineering (electrical, civil, structural, protection, SCADA)
- Procurement of all major equipment per Owner-approved vendor list (Appendix B)
- Construction, testing, and commissioning per IEEE, NESC, and Helix Power Cooperative standards HPC-STD-001 through HPC-STD-012
- As-built documentation and training for Owner operations staff (minimum 40 hours)

## Section 3 - Key Milestones and Liquidated Damages

| Milestone | Target Date | LD Rate (per day) |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Engineering 90% complete | 2026-08-15 | $15,000 |
| Major equipment on site | 2026-11-01 | $25,000 |
| Energization | 2027-02-15 | $35,000 |
| Final acceptance | 2027-03-31 | $20,000 |

Liquidated damages are cumulative, capped at 10% of the contract value ($4,850,000 cap on $48.5M contract).

## Section 4 - Proposal Requirements

Meridian Grid Services submitted Proposal MGS-2026-HPC-042 on 2026-03-20. Proposals must include lump-sum pricing, schedule (Primavera P6 format), safety EMR for past 3 years (MGS EMR: 0.72), and resumes of key personnel. Owner reserves the right to award based on best value, not lowest price.

## Section 5 - Evaluation Criteria

Technical capability (40%), price (30%), schedule (20%), safety record (10%). Reference projects must include at least two 138 kV substations completed within the past 5 years.
