## Section 1 - Change Order Authorization

No work beyond the original Substation Alpha EPC scope shall commence without a signed Change Order (CO) issued by Helix Power Cooperative's authorized representative (Project Manager or designated alternate). Verbal directives do not constitute authorization. Emergency work may proceed with 24-hour written confirmation via MGS Change Request Form CR-001.

## Section 2 - Pricing Methodology

Change Order pricing follows this hierarchy:

1. **Unit prices** listed in Contract Schedule A (if applicable item exists)
2. **Agreed lump sum** negotiated within 10 business days of CR submission
3. **Time and materials** at loaded rates: Engineer $185/hr, Field Supervisor $142/hr, Journeyman Electrician $118/hr, General Laborer $78/hr, plus 12% overhead and 8% fee

Material markup is limited to 10% above net invoice cost. Subcontractor markup is limited to 5%. All supporting invoices and timesheets must accompany the CO package.

## Section 3 - Schedule Impact

Meridian Grid Services shall submit a time impact analysis (TIA) in Primavera P6 format for any CO requesting schedule extension. Owner shall respond within 15 business days. Concurrent delay claims require contemporaneous documentation of critical path impact. Schedule extensions are granted in calendar days; no compensation for idle time unless Owner-caused delay is demonstrated per Section 8.4 of the Master Services Agreement.

## Section 4 - Payment Terms

Approved Change Orders are invoiced on the next monthly progress billing cycle (NET 30 from invoice date). Cumulative change order value shall not exceed 15% of original contract value ($7,275,000) without Board approval from Helix Power Cooperative. CO-001 through CO-003 (totaling $1,240,000) have been executed as of 2026-04-10.

## Section 5 - Dispute Resolution

Unresolved CO pricing disputes shall follow the tiered dispute process: (1) project-level negotiation within 10 days, (2) executive mediation within 30 days, (3) binding arbitration under AAA rules in Raleigh, NC.

**Executed:** Meridian Grid Services - Legal Department; Helix Power Cooperative - General Counsel
