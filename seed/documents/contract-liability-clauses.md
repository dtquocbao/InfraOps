## Article 12 - Indemnification

**12.1 Contractor Indemnity.** Meridian Grid Services shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Helix Power Cooperative, its members, officers, and employees from all claims, damages, losses, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from: (a) bodily injury or death of any person; (b) damage to property; or (c) violation of law, to the extent caused by the negligent acts or omissions of Contractor, its subcontractors, or agents in performance of the Substation Alpha EPC contract.

**12.2 Owner Indemnity.** Helix Power Cooperative shall indemnify Meridian Grid Services for claims arising from (a) Owner-provided design errors in Preliminary Design Package HPC-PD-2025-09, or (b) hazardous conditions at the site known to Owner but not disclosed in writing prior to contract execution.

**12.3 Mutual Limitation.** Neither party shall be liable for consequential, incidental, or punitive damages, including lost profits or loss of power revenue, except for breaches of confidentiality or willful misconduct.

## Article 13 - Insurance Requirements

Contractor shall maintain throughout the project:

| Coverage | Minimum Limit |
|----------|--------------|
| Commercial General Liability | $5,000,000 per occurrence |
| Professional Liability (E&O) | $3,000,000 per claim |
| Workers' Compensation | Statutory limits |
| Umbrella/Excess | $10,000,000 |
| Builder's Risk | Full replacement value ($52M) |

Helix Power Cooperative shall be named additional insured on CGL and umbrella policies. Certificates must be filed 15 days before mobilization to Substation Alpha.

## Article 14 - Limitation of Liability Cap

Except for indemnification obligations under Section 12.1(a), gross negligence, or willful misconduct, total cumulative liability of Meridian Grid Services under this agreement shall not exceed 100% of fees paid or payable under the Substation Alpha contract ($48,500,000).
