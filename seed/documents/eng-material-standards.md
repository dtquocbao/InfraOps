## 1. Purpose

MGS-STD-004 defines minimum material requirements for structural steel used in Substation Alpha equipment support structures, control building framing, cable tray supports, and perimeter fencing. All fabricators shall hold current AISC certification and ISO 9001:2015 registration.

## 2. Material Grades

| Application | ASTM Grade | Minimum Yield (ksi) | Coating |
|-------------|-----------|---------------------|---------|
| Main support columns | A992 | 50 | Hot-dip galvanized |
| Bracing and secondary members | A36 | 36 | Hot-dip galvanized |
| Anchor bolts | F1554 Grade 55 | 55 | Plain or galvanized |
| Base plates | A572 Grade 50 | 50 | Hot-dip galvanized |

Galvanizing thickness shall meet ASTM A123 minimum of 3.9 mils for members ¼ inch to ½ inch thick. Touch-up shall use zinc-rich paint meeting SSPC-Paint 20 Level 1.

## 3. Welding and Fabrication

All structural welding shall conform to AWS D1.1 with welders qualified to 3G and 4G positions minimum. Full-penetration welds are required at column-to-base-plate connections. Non-destructive testing (NDT) by ultrasonic inspection is mandatory for 100% of moment connections and 25% random sampling of fillet welds on bracing.

Fabrication tolerances: member length ±1/16 inch, hole placement ±1/32 inch per AISC Code of Standard Practice Section 6. Camber for beams exceeding 30 feet shall be ¾ inch minimum.

## 4. Inspection and Documentation

Mill test reports (MTRs) are required for every heat lot delivered to the Substation Alpha site. MGS field inspectors shall verify heat numbers against MTRs before erection. Non-conforming material shall be tagged with a red "HOLD" label and quarantined in the designated laydown area (Grid Section C).
